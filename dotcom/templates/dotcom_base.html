Guidelines for Paul Ford, version 2009-08-30

  1. The goal is to look at things. The big world is filled with lies and snakes, but awesome.
  2. Code is part of the story. Executed on the server, it's hidden from the reader. So add sunlight; GPL everything.
  3. It's basically like exercise, or dying in the sun. In that if you stop for three days you're all bloated and sluggish. Stick to the schedule. Websites like this are about ritual.
  4. Don't whore it up. Don't put ads here. If you need scratch that bad then give handjobs or if it's really bad do WordPress installs.
  5. There is no author-reader contract. Don't expect the audience to do anything for you, ever.
  6. But remain open to everything from anyone. But listen to all the anger and criticism and praise and enjoy it.
